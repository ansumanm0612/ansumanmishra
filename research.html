Thesis

  • Mishra, A. (in progress). Causal Architecture, Actuality & Negation in Hindi-Urdu Modality. MA thesis, The English and Foreign Languages University, Hyderabad. Analyses the sak-/pā- modal distinction through causal premise semantics, event-relativity, and two-dimensional projection. Argues for a split between a CAUSE-quantifier (pā-) and a PERMIT-quantifier (sak-), with actuality entailments derived from causal structure under negation.
